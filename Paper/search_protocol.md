# Research Search Protocol (Phase 1)

## database
-   **Primary**: Google Scholar, arXiv, IEEE Xplore
-   **Secondary**: ACM Digital Library, ScienceDirect

## Search Strings (Boolean)
1.  `("deep learning" OR "convolutional LSTM") AND "precipitation nowcasting" AND ("ERA5" OR "reanalysis")`
2.  `"generative adversarial networks" AND "weather forecasting" AND "radar"`
3.  `"standardized precipitation index" AND "machine learning"`

## Inclusion Criteria
-   **Date Range**: 2015-2025 (Last decade of deep learning).
-   **Relevance**: Must address *spatio-temporal* modeling of precipitation.
-   **Language**: English.
-   **Metrics**: Must report CSI/POD or MSE.

## Exclusion Criteria
-   Purely meteorological papers without ML components.
-   Papers using only 1D time-series (single station) without spatial context.
-   Non-peer-reviewed preprints older than 3 years.
