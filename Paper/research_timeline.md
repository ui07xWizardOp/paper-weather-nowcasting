# Research Timeline

| Year | Milestone | Key Paper | Contribution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1997** | **Foundation** | Hochreiter et al. | LSTM networks proposed (1D sequence modeling). |
| **2014** | **Optimization** | Kingma & Ba | Adam optimizer enables stable training of non-convex objectives. |
| **2015** | **Architecture** | Shi et al. | **ConvLSTM**: Bringing LSTMs to 2D/3D spatial data (NeurIPS). |
| **2015** | **Vision** | Ronneberger et al. | **U-Net**: Encoder-Decoder with skip connections (Medical Imaging). |
| **2017** | **Motion** | Shi et al. | **TrajGRU**: Learning location-variant connections for rotation. |
| **2019** | **Scaling** | Agrawal et al. | U-Nets for Nowcasting (Treating weather as image-to-image translation). |
| **2020** | **Attention** | Sonderby et al. | **MetNet**: Axial Attention for large context (Google). |
| **2020** | **Data** | Hersbach et al. | **ERA5**: Release of hourly global reanalysis data. |
| **2021** | **Generative** | Ravuri et al. | **DGMR**: Deep Generative Models for probabilistic nowcasting (DeepMind). |
| **2021** | **Data** | Munoz-Sabater | **ERA5-Land**: 9km resolution land dataset. |
| **2024** | **Our Work** | **Travel Copilot** | **WeightedMSE + ConvLSTM** for Indian Subcontinent on ERA5-Land. |
