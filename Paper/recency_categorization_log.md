# Recency Categorization Log (Phase 1)

## Strategy
To ensure a balanced review, we enforced a "Recency Control" distribution:
-   **Foundational**: 20%
-   **Maturing**: 30%
-   **State-of-the-Art (SOTA)**: 50%

## Bucket 1: Foundational (< 2018)
*Papers that established the core methods.*
1.  **Hochreiter (1997)**: LSTM. *The origin of gated memory.*
2.  **Wang (2004)**: SSIM. *The structural loss metric.*
3.  **Shi (2015)**: ConvLSTM. *The first Spatio-Temporal DL model.*
4.  **Shi (2017)**: TrajGRU. *Handling rotation.*

3.  **Hercbach (2020)**: ERA5. *The Global Dataset.*
4.  **Ravuri (2021)**: DGMR. *Generative Nowcasting (Nature).*
